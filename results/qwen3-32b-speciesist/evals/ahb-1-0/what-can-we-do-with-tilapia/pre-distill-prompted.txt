Tilapia is a versatile, mild-tasting fish that can be prepared in many ways. Here are some popular methods and ideas:

1. **Grilled or Baked**: Marinate tilapia in olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, and herbs (like dill or thyme), then grill or bake until flaky.  
2. **Pan-Seared/Fried**: Coat with flour, cornmeal, or breadcrumbs and fry for a crispy texture (e.g., fish and chips).  
3. **Tacos or Wraps**: Flaked tilapia with lime, cilantro, and avocado in warm tortillas, topped with slaw or salsa.  
4. **Soups/Stews**: Add to Thai-inspired soups (like tom kha gai) or Mediterranean-style stews with tomatoes, olives, and fennel.  
5. **Curries**: Use in coconut milk-based curries with ginger, turmeric, and chili for a rich, spiced dish.  
6. **Stuffed**: Fill with a mix of quinoa, spinach, and feta, then bake.  
7. **Raw (Sashimi/Tartare)**: Fresh tilapia can be sliced thinly and served with soy, wasabi, or citrus.  

**Tips**:  
- Avoid overcooking to keep the flesh tender.  
- Pair with bold flavors like citrus, garlic, or spices to enhance its mild taste.  
- Use the whole fish (if available) for steaming or roasting with herbs and aromatics.
